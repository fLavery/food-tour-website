Meet your guide!

- Giulia Visciani

Official National Tour Guide Giulia Visciani started the Dingle Food Tours because of her love for food and for Ireland. Dingle is where she ended up some years ago and fell in love with the scenery, the people and the Irish produce and foods. She is originally from the Dolomites in the Italian Alps and came to Ireland to work as a volunteer on an organic farm and restaurant in the Dingle Peninsula. Her family thought her how to appreciate and love every food and ingredients... it was at the age of three that she was eating her first spaghetti.

Food Tour

This tour will bring you into different restaurants and shops where you will get the chance to taste and flavour some great local seafood, Irish cheese and whiskey and more. Your guide will introduce the ingredients and the history behind every plate and place. This is a walking tour of Dingle town and is on every Tuesday and Thursday and it starts at 2 pm for approximately 3 or 3,5 hours. We will meet at the Dingle Tourist Office on Dingle Road. 55eu per person

Whiskey Tour

Ireland has a range of whiskeys that are waiting for you to taste and flavour. On this tour, we will share our passion for Irish whiskey with you and you’ll learn about its history and techniques. The tour is a walking tour and starts at 4pm every Friday at the Dingle Whiskey Distillery. Here, we will learn about the production of Dingle Whisky, its ingredients and techniques. We will then walk into Dingle town and explore some other Irish whiskeys in some of the historic pubs of Dingle. The tour is approximately 3 hours long. 65eu per person
